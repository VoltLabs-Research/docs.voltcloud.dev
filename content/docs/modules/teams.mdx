## Overview

Teams are the top-level organizational unit in VOLT. When you create a team, you get an isolated workspace with its own trajectories, clusters, containers, analyses, and member list. Every action in the platform happens within the context of a team.

You can create as many teams as you need — one per research project, one per lab group, or any structure that fits your workflow.

## Creating a Team

1. Open the team selector from the sidebar.
2. Click **Create Team**.
3. Enter a team name.
4. Click **Create**.

You are automatically assigned as the owner of the new team.

## Inviting Members

1. Click the **person-add icon** in the top-right area of the dashboard header.
2. A popover panel opens with two tabs: **Share** and **Invitation Code**.

### Invite by Email (Share tab)

1. In the **Share** tab, enter the person's email address in the input field.
2. Click **Invite**.
3. The invitation appears in the pending list below. You can cancel pending invitations from here.
4. Use the **Copy link** button in the footer to share the invitation link directly.

### Invite by Code (Invitation Code tab)

1. Switch to the **Invitation Code** tab.
2. Click **Generate Invite Code** to create a 5-character code.
3. Share the code or its link with anyone you want to invite — they will join the team automatically when they open it.
4. You can regenerate or delete the code at any time from the three-dot menu.

The invited person receives a notification (email invitations) or joins immediately (code invitations). Once they accept, they appear in the team member list.

## Roles and Permissions

Teams support role-based access control (RBAC). Each team has a set of roles, and each role defines what actions a member can perform.

| Default Role | Permissions |
|---|---|
| **Owner** | Full access — manage members, roles, clusters, and all resources |
| **Admin** | Manage resources and members, but cannot delete the team |
| **Member** | Use all modules (trajectories, analysis, containers, etc.) but cannot manage team settings |

### Custom Roles

You can create custom roles with fine-grained permissions:

1. Go to team settings.
2. Click **Roles**.
3. Click **Create Role**.
4. Name the role and select the permissions you want to grant.
5. Click **Save**.

Assign custom roles to members from the member list.

## API Secret Keys

Teams can generate API secret keys for programmatic access via the [VoltSDK](/docs/open-source/volt-client).

1. Go to team settings.
2. Click **Secret Keys**.
3. Click **Create Key**.
4. Copy the generated key immediately — it is only shown once.

<Callout type="warn">
Secret keys grant full API access on behalf of the team. Store them securely and revoke keys you no longer need.
</Callout>

## Task: Invite a member and assign a role

1. Click the **person-add icon** in the dashboard header.
2. In the **Share** tab, enter the person's email address and click **Invite**.
3. Once they accept, go to team settings and assign them a role (Owner, Admin, Member, or a custom role) from the member list.

**Expected outcome:** The invited person appears in the team member list with the assigned role and can access the team's resources.

## Task: Create an API secret key

1. Go to team settings.
2. Click **Secret Keys**.
3. Click **Create Key**.
4. Copy the key and store it securely.

**Expected outcome:** A new API key appears in the list. You can use it with `voltsdk` or `@voltstack/voltclient` for programmatic access.
