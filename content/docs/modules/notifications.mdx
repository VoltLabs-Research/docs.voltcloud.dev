title: Notifications

Notifications are a personal event stream covering account and team events. They are user-scoped, not team-scoped: each user has an independent feed and unread state.

![Notifications Overview](/assets/overviews/overviews_notifications.png)

## Popover and unread state

A bell icon in the top navigation opens the recent feed, highlights unread items, and links directly to the referenced resource when one is attached.

## What kinds of events appear here

Two events currently produce notifications:

- **Welcome notification** — sent automatically when your account is created, linking to your dashboard.
- **Team invitation** — sent when another user invites you to join a team, linking directly to the invitation so you can accept or decline.

## Managing the feed

Open the popover, follow the links that matter, and mark items as read.

<Callout type="info">
Notifications arrive through the real-time layer and are associated with your account automatically. There is no separate subscription setup required.
</Callout>
