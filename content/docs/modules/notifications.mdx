title: Notifications
description: Follow important user-scoped events across the workspace in real time.
---

Notifications are a personal event stream covering trajectory processing completion, analysis outcomes, invitations, and other workspace events. They are user-scoped, not team-scoped: each user has an independent feed and unread state.

![Notifications Overview](/assets/overviews/overviews_notifications.png)

## Popover and unread state

A bell icon in the top navigation opens the recent feed, highlights unread items, and links directly to the referenced resource when one is attached.

## What kinds of events appear here

Typical events include trajectory completion, analysis completion or failure, SSH import updates, and workspace invitations.

## Managing the feed

Open the popover, follow the links that matter, and mark items as read.

<Callout type="info">
Notifications arrive through the real-time layer and are associated with your account automatically. There is no separate subscription setup required.
</Callout>
